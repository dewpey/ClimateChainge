Our Solution

Storing climate change data on the blockchain is the only way to premanently perserve and publicize information without centralized control.

Simple REST Endpoints

ClimateChainge offers developers and tinkerers a simple ReST endpoint to send and retrieve climate data from anywhere in the world.

Globally Accessibility

Built upon blockchain, ClimateChainge is accessible to every person in the world through multiple distributed nodes.

Decentralized

Given the nature of blockchain, ClimateChainge isn't controlled by a centralized entity. Instead, it's a democratic system collectively run by people like you.

Immutable

Data on ClimateChainge neither can't be change nor deleted because of the nature of blockchain.

Supports Many Datatypes

ClimateChainge can store anything from PDFs, papers, charts, photographs, spreadsheets, etc.

Retain Credit

When you publish information to ClimateChainge, a unique signature is also published allowing you to retain credit for your work.

Installing MultiChain

Mac OSX or Windows

Linux: 
su (enter root password)
cd /tmp
wget http://www.multichain.com/download/multichain-latest.tar.gz
tar -xvzf multichain-latest.tar.gz
cd multichain-latest
mv multichaind multichain-cli multichain-util /usr/local/bin
exit
